Form

If you sell jewelry, you will need sales and contact forms to interact with your customers as they select and place goods in their shopping carts and perhaps contact you for customer service. That will require the use of a third file called Javascript, which is for creating actions on the website. Here are some important forms which can be placed in the HTML and Javascript (JS) files.

Fieldset

When you create a form, the computer needs to know if it’s disparate information, or all part of the same submission. The Fieldset, which wraps around your form, will ensure that everything becomes one submission. It goes in the HTML file, the front at the top, and the closing tag at the bottom.
